Drake's Biography

Aubrey Drake Graham, known simply as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, and actor. He first gained fame for his role on the TV show Degrassi: The Next Generation before transitioning into music.

Early Life

Drake was born in Toronto, Canada, on October 24, 1986. He was raised by his mother, Sandi Graham, and developed a passion for music early on.

Music Career

Drake released his first mixtape in 2006, and his career skyrocketed with hits like "Best I Ever Had" and "Hotline Bling." Today, he is one of the most influential artists in the music industry.
